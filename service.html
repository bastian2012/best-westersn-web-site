our Services

Meticulous attention to details ensures comfort and relaxation at every step in Best Western Hotel. It offers an array of amenities and services designed to make your stay enjoyable. Our team is dedicated to meet your high expectations. Included in our services are the following:

restaurant

services

Best Western Hotel has a smart casual Modern Thai food restaurant and Bar located on the rooftop of The Continent Hotel. Situated on the 39th floor of the hotel overlooking a fabulous cityscape, it features Thai dining that takes it’s inspiration from street food. Mixology at the hotel remains creative with Signature cocktails taking their inspiration from local herbs and spices. Lyrics on the roof adds live music entertainment to your weekend with Friday and Saturday nights offering local bands playing Thai Pop and International tunes.

reception

services

We are ready to arrange you an unforgettable celebration, restaurant staff will take care about all the smolest details: decorate the hall, arrange musicians, agree the celebration menu with the customer, will make all kinds of world cuisine dishes. The restaurant could serve up to 60 persons at the same time. Working hours are from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Breakfast on workdays are form 7:00 AM to 10:30 AM. On weekends from 8:00 AM to 11:00 AM.

free wifi

services

Stay Happy, Stay connected with us. Our hotel understands every guests’ needs and with the growing technology era WiFi is one of the most important services that we provide. Our free WiFi internet service is available 24-hours* in lobby, meeting room, facilities and guest rooms. This Internet browsing service (http and https) enables users to access Gmail, Facebook, book tickets, buy plane tickets, read the news, find out local information and much more. You can access the Free internet service with the speed limit of up to 1.5Mbps.

gymnasium

services

For travelers who want to take in the sights and sounds of Classic music, Best Western Hotel Gymnasium is the perfect choice. From here, guests can enjoy easy access to all that the lively city has to offer. With its convenient location, the hotel offers easy access to the city's must-see destinations. At Best Western Hotel Gymnasium, the excellent service and superior facilities make for an unforgettable stay. This hotel offers numerous on-site facilities to satisfy even the most discerning guest.

safe parking

services

Forget about your car during your stay in Best Western! The hotel provides free parking spaces in a nearby public parking. Our parking service is exclusively free for all bookings from our website or done directly at the hotel. For your convenience, we drive your car to the parking and bring it back on your departure day. We'll reserve a parking space that you can use whenever you want with no time restrictions. Just mention parking when you book and we'll take care of the rest.

luggage

services

The clever folks at leaveyourluggage.com have created a service that is so simple and sensible it is amazing nobody thought of it before. Rather than spending the last day in Haiti dragging suitcases around, visitors can sign up online and have their bags delivered to the airport ready for collection. It is the perfect service for those who don’t want to waste any of their precious time in the city. We took shuttle bus from airport to Termini and drop off point was at the end of Via Magenta. Verey easy to find hotel. Cheaper than taxi.
